# Network Security

Protects systems and data.

Goals:

CIA Triad

Confidentiality

Integrity

Availability

---

# Confidentiality

Prevent unauthorized access.

Examples:

Encryption

VPN

TLS

---

# Integrity

Prevent data modification.

Examples:

Hashing

Checksums

Digital Signatures

---

# Availability

Ensure service remains accessible.

Examples:

Load Balancers

Redundancy

DDoS Protection

---

Used for MITM attacks.

---

# DNS Spoofing

Fake DNS responses.

Victim visits malicious site.

---

Capturing packets.

Tools:

Wireshark

tcpdump

 ↓
Attacker

# DDoS

Distributed Denial of Service
